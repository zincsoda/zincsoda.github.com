Professional Bio

Steve Walsh is Head of Software at Nixplay. He leads a team of engineers, designers and QA folks building the software for Nixplay's fleet of Digital Photo Frames.

Prior to coming to Hong Kong, Steve was the CTO for a startup in the UK called Secure Broadcast - now VML Technologies.

Before that, he was a senior researcher at SAP working on a HANA Enterprise Cloud as well as researching and contributing to Qemu/KVM, Linux Kernel and Openstack.

He has also worked in Trading Technology for NYSE Technologies and IP Video Streaming solutions for Tyco - American Dynamics.
